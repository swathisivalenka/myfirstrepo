 Malla Reddy Engineering College (MREC)   is the parent college of the Malla Reddy Group of Institutions, 
        Hyderabad, Telangana, India  founded by Ch Malla Reddy. 
        The institute was established in 2002, as approved by the AICTE New Delhi ,
         and was affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, 
         Hyderabad (JNTUH).
